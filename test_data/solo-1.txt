For decades, these tapes sat in boxes—thousands of hours of stories, gossip, and forgotten dialects. I used to spend days transcribing just ten minutes of audio. But with modern ASR, the silence is finally breaking. I can index an entire lifetime of memories in an afternoon. What’s more incredible is the diarization; it distinguishes the grandmother’s shaky wisdom from her grandson’s interruptions. We aren’t just saving words anymore; we’re saving the *cadence* of human interaction. We’re giving a searchable voice to the voiceless.
